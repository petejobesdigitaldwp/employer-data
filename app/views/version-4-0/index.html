Find data on employers

This service lets you view information about employers.

It is currently based on HMRC Pay As You Earn (PAYE) data from the 2015/2016 tax year. This data is broken down by local authority district.

You can use the charts and tables in this tool to find information about:

  • The number of employees living in a local authority district by employer
  • Employer salaries by location
  • Growth or reduction of an employer’s workforce living in a location (this measure is experimental)
  • The geographic spread of employers and employees
